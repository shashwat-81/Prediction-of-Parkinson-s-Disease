About Parkinson's Voice AI

Advanced voice analysis for Parkinson's disease screening

AI Technology

Our system uses state-of-the-art Wav2Vec2 neural networks combined with ensemble machine learning to analyze voice patterns associated with Parkinson's disease.

  • Wav2Vec2: Pre-trained deep learning model for audio
  • Ensemble Methods: Multiple AI models for reliability
  • Feature Extraction: 5,376 deep audio features
  • Cross-Validation: Rigorous performance testing

How It Works
Audio Processing

Your voice is processed using advanced signal processing techniques to extract meaningful features.

Feature Extraction

Wav2Vec2 neural network extracts 5,376 deep contextual features from your voice sample.

Ensemble Analysis

Multiple AI models analyze the features and provide consensus predictions with confidence scores.

Training Data

The AI models were trained on a carefully curated dataset:

  • Healthy Controls: 41 voice samples
  • Parkinson's Patients: 40 voice samples
  • Data Augmentation: 3x expansion with audio modifications
  • Validation: Cross-validated performance testing
AI Models Used

Our ensemble includes multiple specialized algorithms:

  • Neural Network: 79.6% accuracy (best)
  • Random Forest: 77.6% accuracy
  • SVM: 75.5% accuracy
  • Logistic Regression: 77.6% accuracy
  • Gradient Boosting: 69.4% accuracy
